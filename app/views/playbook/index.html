Introduction

The DCS Catalogue is a ‘device’ designed to give ‘the Centre’ (in the current form of NHS Digital) a contract with providers of digital services into health and social care. Through this contract we impose Standards, assure solutions against these standards and manage a technical Standards Roadmap, proactively managing quality and supply to customer markets and supporting system level market, interoperability and data strategy.
